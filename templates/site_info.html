The National Water Model (NWM) serves as a powerful tool for generating flow predictions, but having revised and corrected predictions in an accessible format is crucial for a broader audience beyond hydrology experts. By presenting this information in a dashboard format, this project ensures that specialists and the general public can easily interpret and utilize real-time water data to support decision making and risk assessment.

Site Overview

The West Branch Little River is located in Lamoille County, Vermont. It is a stream of the Little River, which drains into the Winooski River Basin. The site is influenced by nearby Mount Mansfield, which affects runoff patterns, seasonal snowmelt, and precipitation.
